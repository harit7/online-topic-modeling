Judo stars win three medals in US

  British Paralympians took a silver and two bronze medals at the Northglenn Judo Championships in Colorado.  
 Athens silver medallist Ian Rose beat American Myles Porter in the play-off for silver in the 100kg category. 
 Four-time Paralympian Simon Jackson won bronze in the 73kg category while there was also a bronze for Welsh star Maxine Ingram in the women's 63kg category. 
 The tournament formed part of the GB team's preparation for the IBSA World Championships in France in June.
