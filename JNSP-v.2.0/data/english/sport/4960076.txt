TeamBath crush Leeds Met Carnegie

  TeamBath eased to an emphatic 71-21 win over Leeds Met Carnegie in the latest round of Netball Superleague matches.  
 Loughborough Lightning kept up the pressure on Bath by beating Welsh side Celtic Dragons 60-24 to remain in second place. 
 Brunel Hurricanes, now without their retired England shooter Abby Teare, stay in third after a tense 43-34 win over Team Northumbria. 
 In the weekend's final game, Mavericks defeated Northern Thunder 59-31.  
 Only two rounds of competition remain in this year's Superleague before the top four teams move through to the play-offs.
