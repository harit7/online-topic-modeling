Decathlon great Dvorak to retire

  Three-time world champion Tomas Dvorak has announced that he will quit decathlon at the end of this season.  
 The 34-year-old Czech,who won world gold in 1997, 1999 and 2001, said: "I've decided to call it a day. You can't go on forever." 
 Dvorak, former world record holder, will bow out at this summer's European Championships in Gothenburg. 
 He said: "If everything goes well I'll be looking at a tally of between 8,300 and 8.400 points."
