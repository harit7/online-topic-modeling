Tanni to make television bow

   Tanni Grey Thompson will star in two BBC Television programmes on Tuesday.  
 The 11-times Paralympics gold medallist, will feature in An Evening with Tanni Grey Thompson at 1205GMT on BBC2. 
 Among those paying tribute to Tanni are Sir Steve Redgrave, Gareth Edwards, Ian Botham and Michael Douglas. 
 That programme will be preceded by a review of the Athens Paralympics at 1145 which will look at the highs and lows of the Games.
