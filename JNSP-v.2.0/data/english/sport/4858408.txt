New NFL season schedule unveiled

  Super Bowl champions Pittsburgh will kick off the 2006-07 NFL season with a home game against Miami on 7 September.  
 The Thursday night clash heralds an exciting opening weekend which sees quarterback brothers Peyton (Colts) and Eli Manning (Giants) face each other. 
 Their Sunday encounter is followed by a double-header on Monday - Washington v Minnesota, and Oakland v San Diego. 
 Thanksgiving Day will feature a live TV triple bill - Detroit v Miami, Dallas v Tampa, and Kansas City v Denver.
