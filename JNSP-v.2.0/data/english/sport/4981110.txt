Nalbandian faces Davydenko test

  Top seed David Nalbandian marched into the final of the Estoril Open with a straight-sets victory over Spaniard Albert Portas on Saturday.  
 The Argentinian, the reigning Masters Cup champion and Estoril winner four years ago, was forced to work hard in the first set before taking it 7-5. 
 He stepped up a gear in the second, taking it 6-1, to book his final berth. 
 Nalbandian will face Nikolay Davydenko in the final after the second seat beat Carlos Moya 3-6 6-2 6-2.
