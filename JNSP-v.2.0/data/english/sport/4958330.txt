Valverde takes stage but not lead

  Alejandro Valverde of Spain won stage four of the Tour de Romandie on Saturday - but it was not enough to move him into the overall lead.  
 The Caisse d'Epargne team rider out-sprinted Switzerland's Alexandre and Australia's Cadel Evans at the end of the 127-km mountain stage. 
 Spaniard Alberto Contador finished in the same time as Valverde and retained the overall race lead. 
 The Tour ends with a 20.4km individual time trial in Lausanne on Sunday.
