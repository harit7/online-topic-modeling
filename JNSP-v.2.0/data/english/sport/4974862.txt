Rochus into Munich quarter-finals

  Olivier Rochus, one of just two seeded players left in the BMW Open, reached the quarter-finals with a 6-2 7-6 (7-6) win over Spain's Ruben Ramirez Hidalgo.  
 The Belgian fifth seed fought off a set point in the tiebreaker and then won the next three points to seal the win. 
 His now plays German qualifier Denis Gremelmayr, who reached his first career quarter-final by beating Austria's Alexander Peya 6-4 6-4. 
 Gremelmayr beat Argentine top seedGuillermo Coria in the first round.  
 Ivo Karlovic hit his 27th ace to complete a comeback 4-6 6-3 7-6 (8-6) victory over Bjorn Phau, and Juergen Melzer beat Simon Greul 6-3 1-6 6-4.
