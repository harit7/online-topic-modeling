Fed Cup promotion eludes Britain

  Great Britain's women lost their Fed Cup promotion play-off against Slovakia and will remain in the competition's Euro Africa Zone Group.  
 The British team of Anne Keothavong, Elena Baltacha, Claire Curran and Katie O'Brien had come through their pool group matches in Plovdiv undefeated. 
 They defeated Ukraine 3-0 before seeing off hosts Bulgaria 2-1 and Hungary 2-1. 
 But the Slovaks, spearheaded by world number 16 Daniela Hantuchova, proved too strong and beat Great Britain 2-0.
