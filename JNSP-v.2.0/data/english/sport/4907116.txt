Cycling on the BBC

           World Track Cycling   Saturday 15 April  BBC One, 1305   Sunday 16 April  BBC Two, 1520       You can watch action from the World Track Championships on Grandstand.  
 Great Britain won ten golds in Los Angeles last year and they are sending a strong squad for the four-day event in Bordeaux. 
 Those competing include Commonwealth gold medallists Rob Hayles, Paul Manning and Victoria Pendleton. 
 You can catch the best of the weekend's action on BBC One at 1305 BST on Saturday 15 April and on Sunday 16 April at 1520 on BBC Two.
