Nieminen advances to semi-finals

  Jarkko Nieminen beat Czech Robin Vik to reach the semi-finals of the BMW Open clay-court event in Munich.  
 The Finn, the third seed and top-ranked player left, will now play Kristof Vliegen after the Belgian beat German Philipp Kohlschreiber 7-5 6-4. 
 Fifth seed Olivier Rochus, the other remaining seed, ended the run of German qualifier Denis Gremelmayr 6-4 7-5. 
 Jurgen Melzer of Austria tamed Croatian Ivo Karlovic 7-6 (7-4) 6-1 to set up a semi-final with Belgian Rochus.
