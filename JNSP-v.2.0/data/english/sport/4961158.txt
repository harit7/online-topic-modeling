Bracciali wins maiden tour title

  Seventh seed Daniele Bracciali of Italy upset top seed Nicolas Massu of Chile 6-1 6-4 to win the Grand Prix Hassan II tournament in Casablanca.  
 Bracciali, who is ranked 68th, beat three seeded players to win the clay-court title and it is the 28-year-old's first ATP singles crown. 
 It is the second time Massu, who was bidding for his seventh title, has lost a final this season. 
 "This win gives me a lot of confidence for the future," said Bracciali.
