Gil reaches Estoril last eight

  Frederico Gil delighted the home crowd with a 4-6 6-4 6-1 upset win against Russian fifth seed Dmitry Tursunov in the second round of the Estoril Open.  
 Top seed David Nalbandian, the 2002 champion, saw off French outsider Jeremy Chardy 6-1 7-6 (7-0). 
 "He started pushing hard and hit a lot of winners," said Nalbandian. "He did a good job and played well to take it to the breaker. I knew it would be tough." 
 Sixth seed Carlos Moya advanced with a 6-1 7-5 win over Gustavo Marcaccio.
