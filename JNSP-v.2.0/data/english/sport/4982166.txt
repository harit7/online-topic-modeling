Coach Grobler is awarded an OBE

  Chief British rowing coach Jurgen Grobler, 59, has been awarded an honorary OBE.  
 The German has been recognised for his contribution to Britain's success since he became involved in 1992. 
 "I am deeply honoured and proud to have received this award", said Grobler, who has coached winning crews at every Olympic Games between 1992 and 2004. 
 As a foreign national, Grobler will receive his award from the Foreign and Commonwealth Office.
