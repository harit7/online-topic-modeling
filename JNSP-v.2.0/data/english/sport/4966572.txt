Willcox claims sixth tennis title

  Peter Willcox and Catherine Graham won the men's and women's titles at the National Deaf Tennis Championships.  
 Defending champion Willcox held off a second-set fightback by top seed Anthony Sinclair to claim his sixth national crown 6-3 7-6 (7-4). 
 Graham clinched her third women's singles title with a 6-3 6-3 win over defending champion Beth Simmons. 
 Sinclair and Willcox took their third men's doubles final, while Graham and Sharon Templeman won the women's final.  
 Meanwhile, the mixed doubles final also brought a third title in four years for the top-seeded partnership of Alex Simmons and Daniel Tunstall.
