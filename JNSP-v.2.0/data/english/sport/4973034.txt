Hereford 3-2 Morecambe (4-3 agg)

  Hereford secured a Conference play-off final spot against Halifax on 20 May after Guy Ipoua's extra-time winner against Morecambe.  
 The tie stood at 1-1 from the first leg and the home side took the lead when Tamika Mkandawire headed home before Wayne Curtis equalised with a penalty. 
 Andy Williams drilled in a shot to restore Hereford's lead but Michael Twiss hauled the visitors level again. 
 But Ipoua fired in after 107 minutes to clinch Hereford a dramatic win.
