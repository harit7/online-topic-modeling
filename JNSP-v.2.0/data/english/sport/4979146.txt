Ex-champions reach Estoril semis

  Former champions David Nalbandian, Nikolay Davydenko and Carlos Moya all reached the semi-finals of the Estoril Open with straight-sets wins on Friday.  
 Top-seed Nalbandian beat Portuguese wildcard Frederico Gil 6-1 6-2 and now meets Albert Portas in the last four. 
 Spaniard Portas sealed a 6-4 6-3 win over American Justin Gimelstob while Russian second seed Davydenko edged out Luxembourg's Gilles Muller 6-4 6-4. 
 He faces Moya, who beat fellow Spaniard Guillermo Garcia-Lopez 7-6 6-4.
