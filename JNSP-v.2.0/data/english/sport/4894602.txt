Harland secures pentathlon bronze

  Britain's Georgina Harland produced a superb run to win a bronze medal in the Women's Modern Pentathlon World Cup meet at Millfield School in Somerset.  
 Harland, who also won bronze at the Athens Olympics in 2004, finished behind winner Aya Medany of Egypt and Viktoriia Tereschuk of the Ukraine. 
 Junior athlete Mhairi Spence finished an impressive seventh overall. 
 Britain's Sam Weale finished 10th behind Dzmitry Meliakh of Belarus in the men's event on Saturday.
