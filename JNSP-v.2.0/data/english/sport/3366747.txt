Freitas claims WBO title

   Brazilian boxer Acelino Freitas captured the WBO lightweight title with a unanimous points decision over Artur Grigorian of Uzbekistan.  
 Freitas had his opponent down in the fourth, sixth, seventh and eighth rounds of the bout in Connecticut. 
 His flamboyant style comfortably overwhelmed Grigorian, who had been out of action for a year with a shoulder injury. 
 Freitas remains unbeaten in his 35 career fights.
